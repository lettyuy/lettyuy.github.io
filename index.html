My Background / About Me
I began my career studying apparel and graphic design, where I learned to think visually, build with intention, and design around the end user. I went on to spend over six years in fashion design and product development, bringing concepts to life and working across the full lifecycle from ideation to launch.
During that time, I became increasingly curious about the data behind product decisions — especially the voice of the customer. I found myself drawn to social listening, customer reviews, and trend signals: What were customers actually saying? What patterns were emerging beneath the surface? How could we use those insights to build better products?
That curiosity led me to pursue a master’s degree in Business Analytics and transition into consulting. Today, I work with a Fortune 1 client focusing on trend intelligence, analyzing large-scale data to surface emerging themes, consumer sentiment, and strategic opportunities.
What I enjoy most is ideating solutions — whether that’s shaping product concepts, building insight tools, or refining systems that make decisions easier and more informed. I’m motivated by solving real problems, improving user experiences, and creating work that is both functional and well-designed.
My background allows me to bridge creativity and analytics: I bring a designer’s empathy, a product developer’s execution mindset, and a data-driven approach to iteration and strategy.
