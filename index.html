My Background

I began my career studying apparel and graphic design, where I learned to think visually, build with intention, and design around the end user. I spent over six years in fashion design and product development, bringing concepts to life from ideation to launch.

Over time, I became increasingly curious about the data behind product decisions — especially the voice of the customer. I found myself drawn to social listening, pattern recognition, and the signals beneath the surface.

That curiosity led me to pursue a master’s degree in Business Analytics and transition into consulting. Today, I work with a Fortune 1 client focusing on trend intelligence and insight-driven strategy.

I enjoy building tools, refining systems, and designing workflows that make decision-making clearer and more effective — blending creative thinking with analytical rigor.
